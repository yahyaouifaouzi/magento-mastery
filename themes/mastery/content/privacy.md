## Information We Collect

When you visit Magento Mastery, we collect basic analytics data (pages visited, time on site, browser type) via Google Analytics. This data is anonymized and used solely to improve the site.

If you submit the contact form, we receive your name, email address, and message. This information is used only to respond to your inquiry and is never shared with third parties.

## Cookies

This site uses cookies for:

- **Analytics**: Google Analytics tracks page views and user behavior in aggregate.
- **Functionality**: A cookie stores your theme preference (dark/light mode).

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some site features may not function as intended.

## Third-Party Services

- **Google Analytics**: Anonymized usage data. [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **Formspree**: Contact form submissions. [Formspree's Privacy Policy](https://formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy/)
- **GitHub Pages**: Site hosting. [GitHub's Privacy Policy](https://docs.github.com/en/github/site-policy/github-privacy-statement)

## Data Retention

Analytics data is retained for 26 months. Contact form submissions are deleted after 12 months or upon your request.

## Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by [contacting me](/contact/).

## Changes

This policy may be updated. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
